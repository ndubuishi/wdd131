Mindful Moments

Website Site Plan

Site Name

Mindful Moments (mindfulmoments.life – available)

This name was chosen because it instantly communicates peace, presence, and personal growth. The word “moments” emphasizes that mindfulness doesn’t require hours — just small, intentional moments throughout the day. The domain feels modern, memorable, and calming.

Site Purpose

Mindful Moments is a personal wellness website that helps visitors reduce stress and build daily mindfulness habits through:

  • Guided breathing, reflection, and gratitude exercises (interactive tools)
  • Short articles on mindfulness science and practical tips
  • A daily mindfulness prompt
  • Progress tracking and gentle email reminders (future feature)
  • Beautiful, calming design that encourages return visits

The site targets busy adults (25–50) who want to feel calmer and more centered but believe they “don’t have time” for traditional meditation.

Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

  • How can I calm my racing mind in under 5 minutes when I’m stressed at work?
  • What are simple daily habits that actually improve mental clarity?
  • Is there a quick way to feel more grateful when I’m having a bad day?
  • How do I start a mindfulness practice without it feeling overwhelming?
  • Can you show me a 60-second breathing exercise I can do anywhere?

Typography

  • Body Text: Inter – clean, highly readable sans-serif
  • Buttons/Accents: Inter Medium (500)

Limited to two fonts for harmony and fast loading.
